Belvoir Hotel offers an excellent collection of well-furnished and contemporary styled accommodation of non-smoking rooms as follows:

Belvoir Classic Room

Our comfortable deluxe room is decorated in a modern style, in soft warm colours. It has large windows, a balcony, and a double-sized bed, which sleeps 1 or 2 persons. Its amenities include air-conditioning, LCD TV, work desk area, tea/coffee making facilities, an elegant bathroom with exclusive bath products and free breakfast served in the restaurant.

Belvoir Executive Room

Our well-designed executive room is decorated in a contemporary style, in soft warm colours. It has large windows, a balcony, and a queen-sized bed, which sleeps 2 persons. Its amenities include a balcony, air-conditioning, LCD TV, work desk area, tea/coffee making facilities, an elegant bathroom with exclusive bath products and free breakfast served in the restaurant.

One-Bedroom Belvoir Suite

Our tastefully finished one-bedroom suite is designed and decorated in a contemporary style, in soft warm colours. The room has a seperate living room and sitting area with large windows, a balcony, and a king-sized bed, which sleeps 2 persons. Its amenities include air-conditioning, LCD TV, Wi-Fi.

Two-Bedroom Belvoir Suite

The Belvoir contemporary two-bedroom suite is well decorated in cool calm tones. It features ample space containing a seperate living/sitting area and 2 confortable bedrooms that sleeps between 2 - 4 persons. The suite has a balcony, air-conditioning, LCD TV, Wi-Fi, mini bar, work desk with adjustable lamp.
